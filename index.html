Problem statement:USC club outreach: Garnet Gate 2.0

Students need an easy and oranized way to find information about organizations and their events.

Affinity Diagram

We divided our diagram into five sections: Problem, Clubs, Events, Features, and Solutions/Details. Our problems are reasons we want to create the app; Clubs are organizations we want to include (broadly); Events will be a catalogued list of events and their details; Features are developments we want to include in our app; Solutions/Details are possible steps to take or misc. detials.
